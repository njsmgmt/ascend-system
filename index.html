How It Works
Three steps. Fully automated.
Set it up once. Let it run every day.
Step 01

Prospect & Connect

Every day Ascend finds targeted decision-makers matching your ideal customer profile and sends blank connect requests automatically — no note, no spam, maximum acceptance rate.

Step 02

Research & Message

The moment someone accepts, Ascend researches live signals — a recent post, a funding round, a product launch — and sends a personalized DM referencing that signal within hours.

Step 03

Track & Follow Up

Every action logs to a live dashboard. Automated follow-up messages go out 5–10 days later to anyone who hasn't responded. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Step 04

Inbound Starts Coming to You

Daily outreach activity signals LinkedIn's algorithm that your profile is active and relevant — generating organic profile views and inbound connection requests from people you never contacted. These warm inbounds convert at a higher rate than cold outreach.
